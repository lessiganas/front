Can You Put Clothes In A Skip?

  • July 5, 2023
  • Skip Hire

Yes! You can put your old, unwanted clothes in a skip. At Bailey’s Skip Hire & Recycling, we donate clothing to charities if the items are not worn down.

Types of Waste: What Can You Put in a Skip?

Before hiring a skip, it’s crucial to understand the types of waste that can safely go into it. Standard items typically include garden waste, household items, and construction debris. 

However, certain items are classified as hazardous waste and are not suitable for skip disposal. This category includes items like gas cylinders, asbestos, tyres, batteries, fridges, medical waste, and paints. These require special disposal methods, ensuring that they don’t pose harm to the environment or human health.

What About Clothes?

So, back to our initial question: can you put clothes in a skip? The short answer is yes, you can. While clothes are not hazardous items, disposing of them in a skip can be harmful to the environment.

Why is this? Clothes that end up in skips may be transported to landfill sites, where they take a long time to decompose. For instance, a synthetic fibre like polyester can take up to 200 years to break down! This leads to an increase in greenhouse gas emissions and contributes to climate change.

At Bailey’s Skip Hire however, we recycle all of our skips contents where possible, and act in the most environmentally-friendly manner. This means your clothes can be given a new lease of life, whether that be through recycling or donation.

Charity Shops

Rather than throwing clothes into a skip, another environmentally-friendly option is to donate them to charity shops. Charity shops can resell your clothes, providing funds for various causes. 

Hire The Right Skip Size

To avoid overfilling your skip with items such as clothes, it’s crucial to choose the right skip size for your needs. Bailey’s Skip Hire & Recycling offers a range of skip sizes to accommodate varying amounts of waste. Our team can provide guidance on the right size for your project, taking into consideration the type and volume of waste you expect to generate.

Effective Waste Management

Effective waste management is not just about hiring a skip and tossing everything into it. It involves understanding the impact of our waste on the environment, recognising the distinction between recyclable and non-recyclable materials, and making informed decisions on waste disposal.

Hire Your Skip Today

Hiring a skip from a reputable provider like Bailey’s Skip Hire & Recycling can make the process sustainable and more efficient. Always remember, proper waste management goes a long way in preserving our environment for future generations.

Get in touch today to book your skip and get rid of your unwanted waste. We will collect the skip and recycle the waste, giving you peace of mind that you are acting sustainably. This is key to reducing our environmental impact and promoting a greener way of living.
